Read full aim for Lyxor International Asset Management		 Aim:
The Lyxor Pan Africa UCITS ETF - C-EUR is a UCITS compliant exchange traded fund that aims to track the benchmark index SGI Pan Africa Net TR.The SGI Pan Africa index attempts to capture the performance of the largest 30 stocks listed in Africa or predominantly exploring African assets. The index is equally exposed to three zones with the largest constituents capped at 10%: South Africa, Northern Africa including Morocco, Egypt and Sub-Sahara excluding South-Africa. The SGI Pan Africa is calculated and published by Standard & Poor's with the index constituents reviewed by Société Générale every six months.Lyxor ETFs are efficient investment vehicles listed on exchange that offer transparent, liquid and low-cost exposure to the underlying benchmark index.
