iShares EURO STOXX 50 - B UCITS ETF (Acc) is an exchange traded fund (ETF) that aims to track the performance of the EURO STOXX 50 Net Return Index as closely as possible. The ETF invests in physical index securities. The EURO STOXX 50 Index offers exposure to 50 of the largest equity securities in the Euro zone. iShares ETFs are transparent, cost-efficient, liquid vehicles that trade on stock exchanges like normal securities.
