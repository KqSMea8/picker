Read full aim for iShares V plc		 Aim:
iShares MSCI Japan Monthly EUR Hedged is an exchange traded fund (ETF) that aims to track the performance of the MSCI Japan 100% Hedged to EUR Index Net as closely as possible. The ETF invests in physical index securities and is currency hedged which enables investors to hedge currency exposure in one transaction, without having to monitor and maintain a currency hedge. The MSCI Japan 100% Hedged to EUR Index Net offers exposure to Japanese stocks which comply with MSCI's size, liquidity, and free float criteria. The index also incorporates a monthly hedge, using a one month forward FX contract, to reduce currency exposure.
