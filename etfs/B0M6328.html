iShares FTSE/EPRA European Property Index Fund is an exchange traded fund (ETF) that aims to track the performance of the FTSE EPRA/NAREIT Developed Europe ex UK Dividend+ Index as closely as possible. The ETF invests in physical index securities. The FTSE EPRA/NAREIT Developed Europe ex UK Dividend+ Index offers exposure to listed real estate companies and Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITS) from developed European countries excluding the UK, which have a one-year forecast dividend yield of 2% or greater. The index is free float market capitalisation weighted.
